1. How can i change or Reset my password?

To change your password, you can locate your profile page from the menu and type in a new password in the password field.
In situations where you can not login you can contact the school Secratery or ICT to assist you with a password reset.

2. How can i print/download my result?

To print your result, you have to open the prefered result before clicking on the print option and from the print preview you can choose to either print or save as pdf.

3. How does the online exit work?

You choose the exit date and a comprehensive reason for the request and submit. To ensure review is been done please inform the school secretary immediately after your request so that she can review it at that instant.
