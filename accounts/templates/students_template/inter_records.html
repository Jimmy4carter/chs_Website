General Information:

  • All Students are to offer 12 academic subjects.
  • All Students must offer all 5-compulsory cross - cutting core subjects (see group A).
  • Students will offer 5 subjects from their field of specialization (see group C, D, and E)
  • Science and Business Students are to offer the one school compulsory subject.
  • Science and Business students will offer one (1) elective outside the field of specialization while humanities offer two (2) elective outside the field of specialization
  • Students are to pick one trade subject from Group F (for No. 5 in Group A).
